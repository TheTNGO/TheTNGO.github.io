What's a "Front-End" tech stack?

The software "front-end" is what the end-user sees and interacts with to get a piece of software to do what they want, also referred to as a "user-interface", or a UI. Any time you're browsing your favorite website or running through the menus of your favorite game, you're interacting with the front-end.

×

What's a "Back-End" tech stack?

Back-end code boils down to the code that handles anything done behind the scenes. From the simple timing of displaying text, to the complex handling of databases, back-end tech stacks tell software and web sites how they work "under the hood".
